Jump Discontinuity/Stealth Mod

I wrote a total conversion of Half-Life 2 that completely revamps the AI and player capabilities to make the game play like a stealth action game in the vein of Deus Ex, Dishonored, etc. Some of the features I coded include:

  • 3D Vision Cones for AI
  • Improved NPC Hearing and Smell Senses
  • New NPC behaviors like ringing alarms and dynamic patrol routes
  • Silent takedowns and drop-down assassinations
  • Player movement upgrades: dash teleportation and translocator

Portal 2: Eidolon

An older Portal 2 mod that introduces a new visual style. It uses Portal 2's VScript system as well as Hammer's IO to add new behavior to existing elements. Highlights include:

  • Simple puzzle concepts new to Portal, like using angled surfaces and stacked boxes to redirect cubes.
  • Visuals, sounds, music, gameplay, and maps all made by one person.
